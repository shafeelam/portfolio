Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?
Simple websites typically take 3–6 weeks. Medium or feature-rich projects, such as e-commerce or custom apps, may take 8–12 weeks depending on scope and content readiness.
Do you offer hosting and maintenance?
Yes. Brandilux provides hosting consultation and fully managed maintenance plans — including performance monitoring, backups, and updates.
Which CMS or tech stack is best?
It depends on the project scale. We recommend WordPress or headless CMS for content-heavy sites, and React or Next.js for high-performance, dynamic applications.
Can clients manage content after launch?
Absolutely. Each project includes CMS training and documentation so you can manage your website independently with ongoing support when needed.
