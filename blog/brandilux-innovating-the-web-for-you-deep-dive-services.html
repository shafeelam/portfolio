Comprehensive Services — What Brandilux Delivers

Brandilux provides modular services that can be engaged individually or as an integrated package depending on client needs. I focus on delivering solutions that are both visually compelling and technically robust.

Custom Web Development

I build websites from the ground up that are fully customized, scalable, and optimized for performance. Every project balances aesthetics with functionality, ensuring seamless experiences on all devices and helping clients effectively engage their audience.

CMS Integration

Managing website content should be effortless. I integrate content management systems like WordPress, Joomla, or headless CMSs such as Strapi or Contentful. These systems allow clients to update content without deep technical knowledge, while providing secure, scalable back-end support.

Web Application Development

For clients requiring interactive or data-driven experiences, I develop responsive web applications. These apps manage complex workflows, offer dynamic functionality, and are designed to grow alongside the business.

API and Backend Integration

Seamless integration with third-party services, payment gateways, or internal databases is critical. I ensure APIs are properly connected, and backend systems are robust and secure, allowing front-end features to function flawlessly.

Web Design

Effective design is a combination of beauty and clarity. I craft user-centered designs with attention to typography, color schemes, and interaction patterns that reflect a brand’s identity and encourage engagement.

Product Design

I create intuitive digital products by understanding user behavior, defining workflows, and designing interfaces that simplify tasks while maintaining elegance and consistency across platforms.

Web Maintenance

Websites require ongoing care. I provide maintenance services including updates, security patches, backups, and performance monitoring to ensure every project continues to operate at peak performance after launch.

Design & Development Process

Brandilux follows a predictable, client-friendly process that balances creativity with delivery discipline:

  1. Discovery & Research: Stakeholder interviews, user persona mapping, competitor analysis, and defining success metrics.
  2. Strategy & Planning: Roadmaps, prioritized features, technical architecture, and content strategy.
  3. Design: Wireframes → prototypes → design system (colors, typography, components).
  4. Development: Iterative sprints, modular components, accessible markup, CMS integration, and automated testing.
  5. QA & Launch: Cross-browser testing, performance benchmarking, accessibility checks, and deployment with rollback plans.
  6. Post-launch & Growth: Analytics review, SEO optimization, A/B testing, and scoped feature enhancements.

Common Challenges & Brandilux Solutions

  • Scope creep / unclear requirements: Clear discovery docs, phased delivery, and weekly stakeholder demos keep scope aligned.
  • Site performance & Core Web Vitals: Optimized images, lazy loading, code splitting, and CDNs ensure fast load times.
  • Security & data protection: Secure deployment pipelines, HTTPS, input validation, role-based access, and scheduled patching.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?
Small sites: 3–6 weeks. Medium sites / e-commerce: 6–12+ weeks. Timeline depends on content readiness and scope.
Do you provide hosting and maintenance?
Yes — Brandilux offers hosting guidance or fully managed hosting + maintenance plans including updates, backups, and security patches.
Which CMS or tech stack should I choose?
It depends on scale: WordPress or headless CMS for content-heavy sites, React/Next for dynamic apps, or serverless/static approaches for performance-first sites.
Can I update the site myself after launch?
Absolutely — CMS training and documentation are provided. For more complex features, ongoing support is available.
