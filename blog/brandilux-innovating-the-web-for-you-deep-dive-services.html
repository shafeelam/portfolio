Comprehensive Services — What Brandilux Delivers

At Brandilux, I provide a complete range of services to help businesses and individuals establish a strong digital presence. Each service is tailored to your unique needs, combining creativity, technical precision, and strategic thinking to deliver measurable results.

1. Custom Web Development

Every business has unique needs, and that’s why I specialize in building websites from scratch that reflect your brand and objectives. I focus on creating fully customized, scalable, and secure websites that perform seamlessly on all devices. From responsive layouts and intuitive navigation to performance optimization, each website is designed to engage users and convert visitors into loyal customers. By combining clean, semantic code with modern frameworks, I ensure your website is not just visually appealing but also future-proof.

2. CMS Integration

Managing content shouldn’t be a technical headache. I integrate user-friendly content management systems, including WordPress, Joomla, or headless CMS solutions like Strapi and Contentful. These systems allow you to update text, images, and pages effortlessly while keeping the backend secure and organized. I also customize CMS workflows so your team can manage content efficiently, ensuring your website stays fresh and relevant without relying on constant developer intervention.

3. Web Application Development

Some projects require more than a traditional website. For those, I build fully functional web applications tailored to your business processes. Whether it’s a customer portal, booking system, or interactive dashboard, I focus on usability, speed, and reliability. Every application is responsive, tested for cross-browser compatibility, and designed to scale as your business grows. I prioritize seamless user experiences, secure data handling, and integration with other tools and platforms.

4. API and Backend Integration

Behind every smooth digital experience is a robust backend. I integrate APIs to connect your website or app with external services, such as payment gateways, CRMs, or third-party platforms. I also design custom backend solutions to manage databases, server logic, and user workflows efficiently. The result is a system where front-end features work flawlessly, data is secure, and business processes are automated, improving productivity and reducing manual effort.

5. Web Design

Design is about more than aesthetics—it’s about communication. I create visually compelling, user-centered web designs that reflect your brand identity and resonate with your audience. From wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes, typography, color palettes, and micro-interactions are carefully chosen to enhance clarity, usability, and engagement. The goal is to craft interfaces that are intuitive, enjoyable, and conversion-focused, ensuring visitors not only stay but also take action.

6. Product Design

Designing a product involves understanding the user and the problem it solves. I focus on creating digital products that are functional, easy to use, and visually coherent. By defining user flows, creating prototypes, and testing interactions, I ensure the product meets real-world needs. Every element—from buttons to menus—is designed to minimize friction, guide users, and make the experience as smooth and delightful as possible.

7. Web Maintenance

Launching a website is just the beginning. I provide ongoing maintenance to keep your digital presence secure, optimized, and up-to-date. This includes regular updates, security patches, backups, performance monitoring, and content tweaks. By proactively maintaining your site, I ensure it continues to function flawlessly, stays relevant in search engines, and adapts to evolving user expectations and business needs.

Design & Development Process

Brandilux follows a predictable, client-friendly process that balances creativity with delivery discipline:

  1. Discovery & Research: Stakeholder interviews, user persona mapping, competitor analysis, and defining success metrics.
  2. Strategy & Planning: Roadmaps, prioritized features, technical architecture, and content strategy.
  3. Design: Wireframes → prototypes → design system (colors, typography, components).
  4. Development: Iterative sprints, modular components, accessible markup, CMS integration, and automated testing.
  5. QA & Launch: Cross-browser testing, performance benchmarking, accessibility checks, and deployment with rollback plans.
  6. Post-launch & Growth: Analytics review, SEO optimization, A/B testing, and scoped feature enhancements.

Common Challenges & Brandilux Solutions

  • Scope creep / unclear requirements: Clear discovery docs, phased delivery, and weekly stakeholder demos keep scope aligned.
  • Site performance & Core Web Vitals: Optimized images, lazy loading, code splitting, and CDNs ensure fast load times.
  • Security & data protection: Secure deployment pipelines, HTTPS, input validation, role-based access, and scheduled patching.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?
Small sites: 3–6 weeks. Medium sites / e-commerce: 6–12+ weeks. Timeline depends on content readiness and scope.
Do you provide hosting and maintenance?
Yes — Brandilux offers hosting guidance or fully managed hosting + maintenance plans including updates, backups, and security patches.
Which CMS or tech stack should I choose?
It depends on scale: WordPress or headless CMS for content-heavy sites, React/Next for dynamic apps, or serverless/static approaches for performance-first sites.
Can I update the site myself after launch?
Absolutely — CMS training and documentation are provided. For more complex features, ongoing support is available.
